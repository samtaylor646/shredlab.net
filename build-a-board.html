Build-a-Board Workshop

Duration 2-3 Hours
Age Range 8+
Skill Level All Levels

About This Workshop

Learn to build and customize your own skateboard from the ground up! This hands-on workshop is perfect for anyone interested in understanding how skateboards work and expressing creativity through board art.

You'll learn how to assemble a complete skateboard, apply grip tape like a pro, install trucks and wheels properly, and create custom artwork for your deck. All materials and tools are provided, and you'll take home your finished board.

Perfect for individuals, groups, birthday parties, or team-building events. No prior experience necessary!

Details

  • Trucks, wheels, and bearings
  • Grip tape and tools
  • Art supplies and guidance
  • Professional instruction
