Event Planning Blueprint

Service Type 3-Phase System
Timeline 3-4 Months
Scope Full Management

End-to-end management for professional skateboard contests and events. We handle logistics, safety, staffing, and execution so you can focus on creating an unforgettable experience.

Phase 1: Pre-Event Planning & Logistics

The foundation for a successful event. We deliver a complete operational plan covering all critical pre-event tasks.

Concept & Scope
Define event format (Street, Vert, etc.), divisions, rules, and judging criteria.
Signed Service Agreement & Project Scope Document
Budget & Sponsorship
Develop detailed budget, create sponsorship deck, and secure initial funding/prizes.
Finalized Budget & Sponsorship List
Venue & Permits
Secure location. Obtain all necessary municipal permits (e.g., insurance, noise, public gathering).
Venue Contract & All Required Permits/Insurance Proof
Date & Timeline
Set the final date and create a master timeline for all pre-event tasks.
Master Event Timeline (Gantt Chart or similar)
Infrastructure
Plan site layout (course, spectator zones, vendors, first aid). Book rentals (Sound, Tents).
Site Map/Layout Diagram
Staffing
Recruit and brief Judges, MC, Scorekeepers, Registration, and Safety teams.
Staff Roster with Assigned Roles and Training Schedule
Registration
Set up online system for waivers, fee collection, and participant check-in flow.
Live Registration Portal & Waiver System
Prizes
Source, organize, and prepare all cash, product, and physical awards.
Prize Inventory and Awards Script
