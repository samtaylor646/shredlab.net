Summer Shred Contest

Date August 15, 2025
Time 12:00 PM - 6:00 PM
Location Downtown Skate Park

About This Event

Kick off the summer with our annual Summer Shred Contest! This exciting event is open to all ages and skill levels, from beginners to seasoned pros. Join us for a day packed with incredible skateboarding, friendly competition, and community spirit.

Whether you're competing or spectating, there's something for everyone at the Summer Shred Contest. Watch amazing tricks, cheer on your favorite skaters, and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of one of the season's biggest skateboarding events.

Prizes will be awarded for best trick, best run, most improved, and more! Don't miss out on this opportunity to showcase your skills, meet fellow skaters, and be part of the action.
